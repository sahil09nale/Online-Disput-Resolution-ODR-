Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ResolveNOW's dispute resolution process.

What types of disputes can ResolveNOW handle?

+

ResolveNOW handles a wide range of disputes including consumer complaints, employment issues, contract disputes, property matters, business conflicts, and family disputes. Our AI system is trained to categorize and process various types of legal conflicts effectively.

How secure is my personal and case information?

+

We employ enterprise-grade security measures including end-to-end encryption, blockchain verification, and secure cloud storage. All data is protected under strict privacy policies and compliance with legal data protection standards. Your information is never shared without explicit consent.

What are the costs involved in using ResolveNOW?

+

ResolveNOW offers transparent, affordable pricing significantly lower than traditional legal proceedings. Basic case submission is free, with mediation services starting at $99. Complex cases may have additional fees, but all costs are disclosed upfront with no hidden charges.

How long does the dispute resolution process take?

+

Most disputes are resolved within 7-14 days, compared to months or years in traditional court systems. Simple cases may resolve in 3-5 days, while complex matters might take up to 30 days. Our AI provides accurate timeline estimates based on case complexity.

Are the resolutions legally binding?

+

Yes, agreements reached through ResolveNOW are legally binding when both parties consent. Our platform generates enforceable contracts with digital signatures and blockchain verification, ensuring legal validity and compliance with applicable laws.

What if the other party doesn't participate?

+

We provide multiple engagement methods including email invitations, SMS notifications, and formal legal notices. If a party remains unresponsive, we can issue default recommendations and provide documentation for traditional legal proceedings if necessary.

How does the AI analysis work?

+

Our AI uses natural language processing to analyze case details, evidence, and legal precedents. It categorizes disputes, assesses complexity, predicts outcomes, and suggests fair settlement options based on thousands of similar cases and legal principles.

Can I have legal representation during the process?

+

Absolutely. Legal professionals can register on our platform and represent clients throughout the process. They have access to specialized tools and can participate in all mediation sessions while leveraging our AI insights for better case preparation.

What happens if I'm not satisfied with the resolution?

+

You're never required to accept a proposed resolution. If mediation doesn't result in an agreement, you retain all rights to pursue traditional legal remedies. We also offer appeal processes and can connect you with legal professionals for further assistance.

Is ResolveNOW available internationally?

+

Currently, ResolveNOW operates primarily in English-speaking jurisdictions with plans for international expansion. We're working on multi-language support and compliance with various international legal frameworks to serve global users.
